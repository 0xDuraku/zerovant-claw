Under the Hood
How It Works
3 layers of intelligence running every 15 minutes
01 — Grid Trading Mechanism
Price Grid
Price range split into 10 levels. BUY orders below current price, SELL orders above.
Auto Cycle
Every time price crosses a grid level, the bot automatically captures the spread profit.
Profit per Grid
Minimum 0.4% spacing per level to cover fees and remain net profitable on each fill.
5 Assets
ETH, SOL, BNB, DOGE, XRP run in parallel for diversification and more profit opportunities.
02 — AI Decision Flow
Market Analysis
Every 15 min: ATR, Bollinger Bands, EMA20/50 to detect trend and volatility regime.
Venice AI
AI decides: rebalance grid, adjust range, or hold based on current market conditions.
Adaptive Range
Grid range adapts — tighter in low volatility, wider when volatility is high.
Multi-Timeframe
Trend confluence across 15m, 1h, 4h. AI only acts when 2 of 3 timeframes agree.
03 — Risk Management
Stop Loss
Grid pauses if asset loses 15% of capital. Auto-resumes after 4h cooldown.
Trailing Stop
Price rises 3%+ from range low — grid shifts up to lock profit and follow trend.
Flash Crash Guard
8% drop in one cycle — all orders cancelled, bot waits for stabilization.
Balance Monitor
Checks USDT balance every 2h. Unexpected 15% drop triggers auto-pause + Telegram alert.
